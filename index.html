Olfactory and fragrance Why do we need these friends?




Whenever you smell a certain fragrance, it can bring back cherished moments from the past and evoke warmth, tranquility, or excitement in your mind. Despite the passage of time, memories through fragrance are indispensable in our lives. It helps us recall memorable moments, loved ones, and various other memories. In other words, fragrance not only stimulates our sense of smell but also serves as a journey taking us back to our memories, even for a few fleeting seconds.
